Application for HR Assistant

You will be an integral part of a small but growing HR team within an ambitious company. You will be responsible for the HR administration as well as supporting and working closely with the HR Manager and wider business when necessary. This role reports to the HR Manager.

Job Tasks

  • Overseeing our PeopleHR portal and maintaining and updating employee personnel files, including right to work.
  • Monitoring and managing our sickness procedure ensuring the formal absence management process is followed and managers have the correct support and guidance available.
  • Managing the recruitment process (posting job adverts, liaising with recruitment agencies, sending out letters to candidates).
  • Assisting with onboarding and off boarding of new employees.
  • Preparing employee paperwork (offer letters, contracts, resignation letters, probationary reviews etc).
  • Supporting the HR Manager on a range of issues including disciplinaries, grievance, capability meetings, welfare meetings, acting as a note taker.
  • Dealing with emails and telephone queries on a day to day basis.

Essential skills and Experience

  • Minimum 1 year plus experience in a similar role.
  • Highly organized, methodical and proactive
  • Experience of providing a high level of customer service and dealing with confidential information.
  • Detail oriented with a high level of accuracy to ensure high quality standards are met.
  • Ability to prioritize and manage own time to meet deadlines.
  • Excellent administration and IT skills.

Desirable skills and Experience

  • Microsoft Teams & Sharepoint/Office 365 experience.
  • PeopleHR experience.

Benefits

  • 28 days holidays in a complete year, including bank holidays (increasing to 31 days after 2 years continuous service and 36 days after 5 years continuous service inclusive of the public/bank holidays).
  • Free on-site parking.
  • An Employee Assistance Programme and trained Mental Health First Aiders.
  • Death in Service (on completion of three months service).
  • Company Pension .
  • £500 CPD Budget (on completion of three months service).
  • Company Sick Pay (on completion of three months service).
  • We Care – 24/7 online GP, mental health support and virtual wellbeing
  • Bereavement Counselling; and
  • Probate Helpline

Job type: Full time or Part time (minimum 30 hours a week).
